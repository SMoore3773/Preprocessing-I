About Homer

I'm Homer, hard working family man and sometimes grifter and entrepreneur. I have a loving wife, Marge, and three children. The daughters (Lisa and Maggie) are good, and the boy... well, he's Bart. I like to drink Duff and enjoy Lard Lad donuts. I am also SMRT certified.

What Homer Can Do

It would be easier to list what Homer can't do. Homer is a father and a provider. Homer has won a Grammy, an Academy Award, a Pulitzer Prize, I've been in space, and saved the town more times than I can count!

Where Homer Works

Homer works as a safety inspector at a nuclear power plant as my day job, but I also moonlight as the driver of Mr. Plow, the manager of country music star Lurleen Lumpkin, and astronaut.
